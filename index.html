Welcome to Smart Pill Reminder

This easy-to-use system helps you take your medicines on time with clear reminders and alerts. Designed specially for elderly users, it ensures you never miss a dose again — keeping you and your loved ones healthy and safe.

How It Works

1️⃣ Add your medicine name and the time you need to take it.

2️⃣ The system stores your schedule securely in your browser.

3️⃣ When it's time, you’ll receive a gentle alert and sound reminder.

4️⃣ You can view or clear all your reminders anytime.
